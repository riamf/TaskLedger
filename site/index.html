Features

Built for everyday tracking without spreadsheet overhead.

Flexible task setup

Create one-time or repeating tasks with notes, amounts, durations, and reminders.

Fast daily workflow

Use the daily view to review what matters today, mark items done, and move across dates quickly.

Monthly summaries

Review time, counts, income, and costs in a monthly summary with calendar-based drill-down.

Designed for iPhone

TaskLedger is an iOS app with local notifications, English and Polish support, and CloudKit sync.

Workflow

A simple loop from planning to review.

1

Create tasks

Define recurring or one-time activities and choose the type that matches what you want to track.

2

Log progress daily

3

Review each month

Open the summary tab to understand trends, totals, and the days that shaped the month.
